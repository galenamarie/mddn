The infrastructure of Wellington enables the people of our city to connect to other people. There are many telltale signs of electricity and connection throughout the city's streets, though these often tend to blend into the urban landscape. If you look skywards, to the ground, or even directly ahead, you are quite likely to find evidence of the physical infrastructure that connects Wellingtonians to the wider worldwide network.

Handholes

Hand holes are shallow holes in the ground that provide access to a top row of ducts in an electrical system. These are found all over Wellington's sidewalks and exist under a variety of company names.

CCTV Cameras

CCTV cameras (also known as closed-circuit television or video surveillance) are often hard to spot if you aren't looking for them. Only some are hidden, but most are visible in Wellington outside the main doors of the cities' restaurants and retail stores.
